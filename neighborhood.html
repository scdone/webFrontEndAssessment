About

Mesa, Arizona is a part of the greater Phoenix Metropolitan area, also known as "The Valley." (You may have noticed "The Valley" jerseys on The Suns in the playoffs this year.) Mesa is located 20 miles from the heart of downtown Phoenix and has a population of approximately 500,000 people.
